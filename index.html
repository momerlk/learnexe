Bridging societal gaps, spreading digital literacy, and using tech to solve problems that matter.

We at Learn.exe have been working for the past year to promote education and digital literacy within pakistan especially amongst those that are unable to access this information.

To do this we have recruited volunteers, spread awareness and also created our very own educational tablet from the ground up. Moreover, we have worked with nationally-acclaimed organisations to teach coding and STEM to children in classrooms across Pakistan.

Not only that but, we have also applied technology to solve various problems that plague our society. Like a crime control project with the national police as well as an accessibility device for the deaf and dumb allowing them to communicate with people regardless of whether they know sign language.

Target Groups

We are a not-for-profit aiming to promote digital literacy standards in Pakistan among three major target groups:

Neglected communities, like women and khawaja saras.
Underprivileged people who do not have access to the necessary resources.
People with disabilities .

Wings of the Organisation

Parwaaz-e-Aam

Education for all people, no matter their financial, religious or social standing. We believe in dispersing digital literacy, as well as knowledge about programming and other essential fields related to computer science for the good of the people.

Inqalaab

Underdeveloped countries face alot of challenges and problems on a very large scale. Problems that have not been solved by the government or traditional means. We believe technology and its application can greatly change this and solve these problems which is what we hope to achieve in this wing.

Tazeem-e-Khatoon

Amidst the stark reality of a lack of education and resources for women in Pakistan, as well as cultural restrictions on them, we want to be a vessel to teach these women essential computer skills to open up opportunities for them, whether that be freelancing, remote work or work from home.

Himmat-e-Maazoor

One of the most neglected groups in the country, maazoors(the disabled) have no proper support system to give them equal chances to excel in life, to learn and to grow. We plan to change this, with programs targeted towards such communities in order to allow them to achieve success in the field of computer science.
